## How Points Work

### Earning Points

Points are earned automatically for every swap you make on ChameleonSwap. The system tracks your trading activity and awards points based on:

- **Trading Volume (USD)** - The total value of your swaps in USD
- **Weekly Distribution** - Points are distributed weekly based on your trading volume from the previous week
- **Proportional Allocation** - Points are distributed proportionally to all traders based on their share of total weekly volume

### Weekly Distribution

Points are distributed every week based on the previous week's trading activity:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Trading Period">
    All swaps made during a calendar week (Monday-Sunday, UTC) are tracked.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Volume Calculation">
    Your total trading volume in USD is calculated by summing all swap values (`notionalUsd`) from that week.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Point Allocation">
    A fixed pool of points (default: 1,000,000 points) is distributed proportionally:
    - Your share = (Your USD Volume / Total USD Volume) Ã— Total Points Pool
    - Points are rounded to whole numbers
  </Step>

  <Step title="Distribution">
    Points are credited to your account and appear in your points balance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Points are distributed automatically by the system. You don't need to claim them - they appear in your account after distribution.
</Note>

## Leaderboard

The leaderboard ranks all traders based on their total points. Compete with other users to reach the top!

### How the Leaderboard Works

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ranking System">
    Traders are ranked by:
    1. **Total Points** - Cumulative points from all weekly distributions
    2. **Tie-breaker** - If points are equal, ranking may consider total USD volume
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Top 10 Display">
    The leaderboard shows the top 10 traders on the platform:
    - Rank number (1-10)
    - Wallet address (shortened)
    - Total points
    - Total USD volume
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Your Rank">
    Your rank is displayed prominently on the Points page:
    - Shows your position if you're in the top rankings
    - Updates automatically as rankings change
    - Includes your total points and USD volume
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Leaderboard Features

The leaderboard displays:
- **Rank** - Position number (1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.)
- **Address** - Wallet address (shortened for privacy)
- **Points** - Total cumulative points
- **Total Value (USD)** - Total trading volume in USD

<Note>
  The leaderboard updates after each weekly distribution. Rankings may change as new points are distributed.
</Note>

## Referral Bonuses

Earn bonus points through the referral system! When you invite friends or are invited by someone, you can earn additional points.

### Types of Referral Bonuses

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Inviter Bonus" icon="gift">
    Earn points when users you've invited make swaps. You receive a percentage of their trading volume as bonus points.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Invitee Bonus" icon="star">
    Earn bonus points when you're invited by someone and make your first swaps. A welcome bonus for new users!
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### How Referral Bonuses Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate Referral Code">
    Create your referral code after reaching 1,000 USD in trading volume. See the [Referrals guide](/referrals) for details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share Your Code">
    Share your referral code or link with friends. When they use it, they become your invitees.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Earn Inviter Bonus">
    When your invitees make swaps, you earn bonus points based on their trading volume. These points are added to your total.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Earn Invitee Bonus">
    If you were invited by someone, you may receive bonus points for your initial swaps as a welcome bonus.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing Referral Bonuses

On the Points page, you'll see:
- **Total Points** - May include a breakdown showing points from referrals
- **Referrals Volume** - Total USD volume from users you've invited
- **Bonus Breakdown** - Separate display of inviter and invitee bonuses (if applicable)

<Note>
  Referral bonuses are distributed along with weekly point distributions. They appear in your total points balance.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How are points calculated?">
    Points are distributed weekly based on your trading volume in USD. A fixed pool of points (default: 1,000,000) is divided proportionally among all traders based on their share of total weekly volume.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When are points distributed?">
    Points are distributed weekly, typically after the end of each calendar week (Monday-Sunday, UTC). Distribution timing may vary.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to claim my points?">
    No, points are automatically credited to your account after distribution. You don't need to claim them.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I lose points?">
    Points are cumulative and don't decrease. However, your rank may change if other traders earn more points than you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I don't trade for a week?">
    You won't earn new points for that week, but your existing points remain. Your rank may decrease as other traders earn points.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do referral bonuses work?">
    When users you've invited make swaps, you earn bonus points based on their trading volume. These are added to your total points during weekly distributions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see my points history?">
    The Points page shows your total points and current status. Individual weekly distributions are reflected in your cumulative total.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between points and USD volume?">
    USD volume is your total trading value. Points are rewards based on that volume, distributed proportionally each week.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
